Available Methods

  • $('#trigger').lightBox().open() Open lightBox
  • $('#trigger').lightBox().close() Close lightBox
  • $.fn.lightBox.close() Close topmost lightBox
  • $.fn.lightBox.closeAll() Close all lightBox
